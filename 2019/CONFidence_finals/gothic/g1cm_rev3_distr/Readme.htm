2. More about Gothic
        1. Once you lay down in a bed you will heal all damage and you can choose how much time you want to bypass.
        2. If an Item can be picked up, it will be highlighted when in Focus.
        3. Objects in the Focus which are useable will be shown with a Name.
        4. To take a look behind you use the sepcial camera by pressing 'R' or 'NUMPAD0'.
        5. Gothic is optimised for DirectX 8 therefore please use the latest DirectX version.
        6. If for any reason Gothic should 'hang', press Alt-Strg-F8
        7. At certain times we do not want you to open the menu. Therefor we disabled this funktion. If for any reason you need to access the menu press Shift-ESC. Anyway you won't be able to save.
        

a) GOTHIC

1. Minimum Requirements

    GOTHIC needs at least:
    Windows 98/2000
    128 MB RAM
    16 MB Video Card
    Pentium 400 (and compatible)
    1 GB HardDisk
    DirectX Soundcard (e.g. Sound Blaster)

2. Installation

    Befor you start the installation of Gothic make sure you already installed DirectX 7a or higher.

    To install Gothic follow the instructions given. Once completed you can find a link on your desktop. To start just click on the link.


3. Short Take

    GOTHIC is a RPG. The camera shows the hero fom behind. How far you want to be behind the hero can be adjusted by pressing the +/- (NumBlock).
    GOTHIC is located in a medival world. You are an imprisioned criminal. In these days the Orcs are at war with the kingdom.  The only hope for the king are the magic ore mines. All criminals are forced to work in the mines.
    The mines are not guarded but the way out is blocked by a magic barrier only passable from the outside but not from the inside.
    Your job is to get through. But first things first. To find your way through GOTHIC you got to talk to some people to gather information.
    A first hint is that there are 3 camps: the old camp, the new camp and the psi camp. In this Demo you will be able to play a part of the surface level.

4. Trading

    The TradingScreen is a special setup of the inventory. On the left hand side you will see the inventory of the merchant (victim). On the right hand side the inventory of your hero.
    If you find another 2 rows in the middle, you are trading with a merchant otherwise you are about to loot a victim. Take what ever you want from a victim with the Action Right combination.
    Dealing with a merchant means you have to pay for what you take from him. The items you can see in his inventory are those he wants to trade, so feel free. On top of this you will see the amount of ore you have to give in exchange. The deal can only be done if you offer at least as much as you want to take. So switch to your inventory (right-key without the action-key) and deal with the Actionkey + right-key combination.
    Press Enter to accept the deal.

    II Sound Cards:
     ----------------

      Usually all Windows compatible Sound Cards should work with GOTHIC. If there are problems please try new drivers first.
              
      If GOTHIC doesn't start try to edit the GOTHIC.INI by changing  "sndEnabled" from "1" to "0"

    III. Performance Tuning

      a) for everyone:

        End all tasks. Other tasks could influence the stability of GOTHIC
                
        Defragmant your harddisk for maximum harddiskspeed. If your harddisk supports UDMA make sure it is activated.
                        
        Do not use comprimised drives (DriveSpace...). These programs need a lot of time to decompress data.
                        
        If you want to upgrade your system try theese things first:

          a) RAM: Instead of 128MB use 256MB for a real increase in speed.
          b) Prozessor:   With at least a K6-400 lots of RAM and a good graphics accelrator, you will be able to play Gothic.
          c) Video card: A lot of Texturememory is important for a good fillrate.

                        
        Play with the Settings in the Video Menu for best results..
                        
        Try www.tweakfiles.com for further help in tweaking.

      b) Operating System

        - Windows 2000

          Though GOTHIC works fine with Windows 2000, we recommend to use Windows 98 (driver suport is more up to date)
          If you use Windows 2000, you should use the Service Pack 2 to use the full AGP.

        - Windows 95

          With Windows 95 it is almost impossible to play GOTHIC

        - Windows NT

          GOTHIC does not work with Windows NT

      c) Graphic Cards

        Your Graphic Card needs more than 8 MB Texturememory and a well supported AGP. Make sure you are using the best AGP drivers for your Motherboard. If you are using a Motherboard with the VIA Chip try the following homepage: www.viatech.com.
