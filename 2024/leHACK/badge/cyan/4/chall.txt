In the 1980s, while working at the Abalon Club, Gordan invented a time machine capable of taking him to any period between 1970 and 2038. Once the machine was completed, on 25 June 1988, he set it off at precisely 6.37pm.

By a happy coincidence, his machine reappeared at the opening of leHack 2024, at the Cité des sciences. A photo immortalised the moment.

The problem is that during this journey, the code that makes time travel possible has been corrupted. To return to his own time, the code has to be found.
